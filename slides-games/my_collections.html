⚠️ Unfortunatly, after the new Google Slides update, Job Simulator does not work anymore. Fortunatly, I've found a way to fix this. Firstly, you can tell if you have the update by noticing if there is the border of a black box whenever you click a link (button). If you do, the game will only work if you play it right after refreshing your screen. So, refresh this page then immediately start playing this. The black border will show up for the first slide, but it won't for any other slides.
